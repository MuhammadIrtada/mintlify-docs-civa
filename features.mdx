title: "Civa Vault Features & Utilities"

# **1. Text-to-Video**

- Convert written scripts into fully animated videos.
- Ideal for content creators, educators, and advertisers.

# **2. Image-to-Video**

- Transform static images into dynamic video sequences.
- Perfect for digital artists, NFT creators, and marketers.

# **3. AI Narration & Sound Effects**

- Automatically generate human-like voiceovers from text.
- Add contextual sound effects for immersive videos.

# **4. Customizable Video Styles**

- Choose from multiple templates, aspect ratios, and animation styles.
